Frequently Asked Questions

×

How do I report a water complaint?

Click on "Add Complaint" in the sidebar, fill out the form with details about the issue, and submit.

How can I track my reports?

Go to "My Reports" to see all your submitted complaints and their current status.

What information do I need to provide?

You'll need to provide a description of the issue, location, and optionally upload photos.

How long does it take to resolve complaints?

Resolution time varies depending on the complexity of the issue. You'll receive updates on the status.
