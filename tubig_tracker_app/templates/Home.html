Welcome to Tubig Tracker

Tubig Tracker is a community-driven monitoring system designed to help identify and track water-related concerns such as leaks, low pressure, and supply interruptions. Built with privacy and efficiency in mind, the platform enables real-time updates and promotes faster response within your community.

Features

Discover the powerful features that make Tubig Tracker the ultimate solution for water issue management

Real-Time Issue Reporting

Users can instantly report water-related issues such as leaks, low pressure, or no supply using an easy-to-use form.

Live Map Visualization

All submitted reports are displayed on an interactive map so the community and administrators can see affected areas in real time.

Status Tracking

Every report has a status—Pending, In Progress, Completed, or False—so users know the progress of their issue.

Admin Management Panel

Administrators can review reports, update statuses, verify issues, and manage user submissions efficiently.

Photo Evidence Upload

Users can attach images to support their reports for better accuracy and validation.

User Accounts & Authentication

Secure login ensures only verified users can submit or track issues.

How It Works

Simple steps to report and track water issues in your community

1

Submit a Report

Users fill out a simple form with issue details such as location, issue type, and optional photo evidence.

2

System Records the Issue

The system saves the report and displays it on the live map for visibility.

3

Admin Reviews

Administrators receive the report, validate the details, and update the status accordingly.

4

Status Updates

Users are notified of any progress such as "In Progress" or "Completed."

5

Community Awareness

Other residents can view ongoing issues on the map, helping the community stay informed.

About The System

This system is a community-powered water issue reporting platform designed to improve communication between residents and local water authorities. It aims to make reporting easier, speed up response times, and keep everyone updated about water-related concerns.

By combining real-time mapping, responsive management tools, and user-friendly reporting, the system helps create a more efficient, transparent, and connected community.
