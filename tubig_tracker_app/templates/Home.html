Welcome to Tubig Tracker

Tubig Tracker is a community-driven monitoring system designed to help identify and track water-related concerns such as leaks, low pressure, and supply interruptions. Built with privacy and efficiency in mind, the platform enables real-time updates and promotes faster response within your community.

Features

Key features for efficient water issue management

Real-Time Issue Reporting

Instantly report water issues like leaks, low pressure, or no supply.

Live Map Visualization

View all reports on an interactive map in real time.

Status Tracking

Photo Evidence Upload

User Accounts & Authentication

How It Works

Simple steps to report and track water issues

1

Submit a Report

Fill out a form with location, issue type, and optional photo.

2

System Records the Issue

System saves report and displays it on the live map.

3

Admin Reviews

Admins receive, validate, and update report status.

4

Status Updates

Users get notified of progress updates.

5

Community Awareness

Community stays informed through the live map.
